About Me

I was born and raised in St. Petersburg, Florida alongside my brother and sister. Growing up I always had a passion for the outdoors and caring for the community. Eventually, after highschool, I went to FSU to study environmental sustainability with the hopes to pursue my childhood passion. After a few years in college I realised that even though my concern for the environment had not faded, it was no longer what I wanted to pursue in life. I now believe that this course is the key to starting down my new path and I can't wait to see where it takes me. During my free time I enjoy golfing, fishing, and playing xbox with my friends. For me golfing is the best way to have fun, challenge yourself, relax, and hangout with your friends all at the same time. Hopefully one day I'll be able to use this program and the golf course to make lifelong friends and business partners.
